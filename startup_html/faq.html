    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is your return policy?

    Our return policy allows for returns within 30 days of the purchase date. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. Please contact our customer support team to initiate a return.

    Do you offer international shipping?

    Yes, we offer international shipping to customers around the world. Shipping rates and delivery times may vary depending on your location. You can find more details during the checkout process.

    How often do you post original paintings for sale?

    We regularly update our inventory with new original paintings. The frequency of new listings may vary, but we strive to offer fresh and exciting artwork to our customers on a regular basis. Be sure to check our "Inventory" page for the latest additions.

    Who owns the rights to your artwork?

    All rights to the artwork created by Krecia Fullmer are owned by the artist. Reproduction, distribution, or any other use of the artwork without explicit permission from the artist is prohibited.
